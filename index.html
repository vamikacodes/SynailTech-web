About Us

The story behind Synail Tech

Our Story

At Synail Tech, we believe nails are more than just beauty — they're a form of personal expression. From the beginning, our focus has been on creating intricate, custom designs that reflect each client's unique style. Whether we're building a set inspired by your mood board or bringing a specific theme to life, we're here to make sure your nails are not only pretty, but also practical and wearable in your everyday life.

Every set we craft is designed to help you feel confident, stylish, and seen; because at Synail Tech, your nails aren't just an accessory; they're a statement of who you are.

Founder's Story

Hi, I'm Syna! Ever since fifth grade, I've loved doing my own nails with regular nail polish at home. I grew up watching countless YouTube tutorials and experimenting with different designs I was always the "art girl" in school! Eventually, I reached a point where I wanted to turn this passion into something bigger, something that truly resonated with me and that's how Synail Tech was born.

When someone walks through my door, it's not just about beautiful nails — it's about creating a genuine connection and offering a space where clients feel they belong.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long do press-on nails last?

With proper application and care, our press-on nails can last 1-2 weeks. For maximum longevity, make sure your natural nails are clean and oil-free before application, and wear gloves when doing household chores.

How do I prepare for my nail appointment?

Please arrive with clean nails free of any polish. If you're getting extensions, it's best to have your natural nails at a manageable length. Also, bring some design inspiration if you have specific ideas in mind!

What's your cancellation policy?

Appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled at least 24 hours in advance without any penalty. For cancellations within 24 hours, a 50% fee may apply.

Do you offer custom nail designs?

Absolutely! For custom press-on nails, you can select the "Custom Nail Set" option and upload a reference image or describe your desired design. For in-person services, just let us know your design ideas during your appointment.

How do I find my correct nail size?

We offer press-on nails in small, medium, and large sizes. If you're unsure of your size, measure the width of your natural nails at their widest point. Small fits nails up to 7mm wide, medium fits 7-10mm, and large fits 10-13mm.
